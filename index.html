The Solution / Owning Orbit 03 / 08

One core capability. Six ways to sell it.

Fully autonomous servicing robots that rendezvous with and capture objects never built to be touched — performing, on station and without a human in the loop:

01

Life Extension

Refuel the host, or dock as a propulsion module that flies it for years.

02

Rescue & Relocation

Tug a stranded satellite to the orbit it was meant to reach.

03

Repair

Free a stuck array or antenna; swap modular payloads.

04

Inspection

Diagnose anomalies up close — a data product in its own right.

05

Debris Removal

Capture dead objects and deorbit or graveyard them.

06

In-Space Assembly

Build structures too large to launch in one piece.

Why Now / Owning Orbit 04 / 08

The window opened — and didn't exist a decade ago.

01

Reusable heavy-lift cut cost per kilogram ~10×. The unit economics finally close.

02

Modern perception and onboard compute make real-time non-cooperative capture tractable.

03

Data centers reach orbit

Space-based data centers are beginning to proliferate — a new class of large, high-value assets needing servicing and upkeep.

04

Rules became revenue

Debris-mitigation mandates turn disposal into a budgeted line item.
